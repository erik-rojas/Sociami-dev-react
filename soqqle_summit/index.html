About the Event

How it All Started

It's no secret. We are in the beginning of the 4th industrial revolution.

Imagine if you could unlock your potential and flourish in the upcoming Human Renaissance. Imagine if you could be placed at the right place, at the right time and with the right people to get you there.

What kind of life will you lead if you could discover your role and cast a foothold in this crucial time? How will your life change if you could achieve all that?

The goal of the Youth for Future Summit is to accomplish that. A monumental program of self-discovery, motivation, and a starter-kit to help you push your boundaries.

Time to Soar

What to Expect from Event

Experiences Reinvented

Interact with trailblazers in disruptive technologies and join case-studies to speed up growth.

Insatiable Knowledge

Review the potential of these technologies, how to dig deeper and create sure-fire solutions.

Exploit Emerging Technology

Join or create a team in a "Ragnarok" Hackathon to challenge your innovation. Create a prototype with your team, in 2 days! Spiral yourself into the Spotlight!

Meet like-minded people who are interested in the same topic as you.

frequently asked qns

General Questions

Topic: Whats the difference between Youth for Future and Ragnarok

Answer: The Youth for Future Summits are preparation events prior to the Ragnarok Hackathon. However you can still go for the summit without attending Ragnarok.

Topic: When and Where will the Event take Place?

The Youth For Future Summit and Ragnarok Hackathon will be held in Nanyang Technological University on March 22nd and May 18-20th in the Student Activity Centre (SAC).

Topic: I am not a Student, can I attend?

The event is focused for students, however there is nothing stopping a non-student from attending.

Topic: How can my company sponsor this event?

Thank you for the interest! Please send an email to talk@soqqle.com

Topic: What is this SOQQ Token?

It is the in-game token for the Soqqle Gamified Learning platform. It is used to power-up the character for learning to obtain cool challenges and items.

Youth for Future

Topic: Do i really get 20 Soqq Tokens? How does it work?

The email you registered with will be sent a voucher that can be used to redeem the tokens in the Soqqle platform in BETA. We encourage you to sign up for the ALPHA version now. www.soqqle.com

Topic: What are these workshops in the rundown?

They are designed to help you brainstorm opportunities in the domain of choice. It is also used to help you form a team for the Ragnarok Hackathon should you choose to go for it.

Ragnarok Hackathon

Topic: IS there a requirement for joining Ragnarok?

You have to go for at least ONE Youth for Future summit. Exceptions are on a case by case basis.

Topic: Will i really win 500 Tokens as a Prize?

Yes! This will be given as a voucher for you to redeem in the system.

Topic: What is the max team size?

5 team members

Topic: How do I form a team?

It is recommended for a team to have a mix of tech and non tech students. You can either form the team during the youth for future summits or you can form the team on your own.

Topic: Most all members of a team be in the same school?

No, it can be mixed.

Topic: What is the goal of the mentoring during the event?

It is to provide feedback on the solutions proposed and suggest any improvements.
